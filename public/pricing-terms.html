Token credits

AI usage is billed via credits. Each subscription tier includes a monthly credit allowance (amount TBD). When you exhaust your monthly allowance, you can top up with one-time credit packages:

  • $25 in credits — one-time charge
  • $50 in credits — one-time charge
  • $100 in credits — one-time charge

Credits are denominated in dollars and burn down at our metered AI usage rate as you use the platform. The metered rate is a fixed markup on the underlying AI provider's cost — RevPlanner does not separately charge for compute, infrastructure, or storage tied to AI usage. Credits never expire as long as your subscription remains active.

If your subscription is cancelled, unused credits expire 30 days after cancellation.

Refund policy

Subscription fees are non-refundable for partial months. You may cancel at any time via the customer portal; you retain access until the end of the billing period. Credits purchased separately are refundable within 14 days of purchase only if no usage has burned against them.

Plan changes

You may upgrade or downgrade your subscription tier from the customer portal. Upgrades take effect immediately with a pro-rated charge. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Failed payments

If a subscription renewal payment fails, we will retry per Chargebee's dunning schedule (3 retries over 14 days). If all retries fail, your subscription enters a grace period and access is suspended after 7 additional days. You will receive email notifications at each step.

Taxes

Prices listed are exclusive of applicable taxes. Sales tax (where applicable) is calculated and added at checkout based on your billing address.
