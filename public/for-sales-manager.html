The manager's bind

A full book of reps, half an hour each, and a hunch about where to spend it.

The CRM gives you the numbers, never which conversation to have. So you end up coaching whoever is loudest, or whoever's deal just blew up, instead of where the dollars actually are. The rep quietly slipping does not raise a hand. The objection costing you three deals a quarter never shows up as one problem. By the time it is obvious, the quarter is half gone.
