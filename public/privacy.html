Privacy Policy

Effective: June 4, 2026 · ← Back to RevPlanner

RevPlanner is operated by DLEA.IO LLC d/b/a RevPlanner, a Delaware limited liability company. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Service.

1. What we collect

When you create an account: name, email, company, and (during checkout) billing details handled by Stripe — we do not store card numbers. When you connect data sources: CRM records, call recordings, and knowledge base content you authorize us to access. When you use the Service: usage logs, generated content, and product analytics.

2. How we use it

To provide the Service: read your data, generate enablement content, coach your reps, and surface pipeline insights. To bill you: process subscription payments via Stripe and Chargebee. To improve the Service: aggregate, anonymized usage analytics. We do not sell your data.

4. AI processing

Content you upload or connect to RevPlanner is sent to our AI providers (Anthropic Claude, and OpenAI for certain features) for inference. Under our commercial agreements with these providers, your data is not used to train their models. Inference is performed in U.S. data centers. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. If you require regional data residency (e.g., EU-only processing), please contact us — this is a roadmap capability rather than a current commitment.

5. Data retention

Account data: retained while your subscription is active and for 90 days after cancellation, then deleted. Generated content: retained as long as your subscription is active; you can export at any time. Logs: 30 days. Backups: 35 days.

6. Your rights

You can request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal data by emailing dan@revplanner.io. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30–45 days). Residents of jurisdictions with applicable privacy laws — including but not limited to California (CCPA / CPRA), Colorado (CPA), Virginia (VCDPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA), the EU and UK (GDPR / UK GDPR), and other states with comprehensive privacy legislation — have additional rights under those laws. We honor verifiable consumer requests as required by the applicable statute, including rights of access, deletion, correction, opt-out of sale/sharing, and limitation of sensitive data processing. To exercise these rights, email us at the address above and identify the jurisdiction whose law you are invoking.

7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest using industry-standard encryption (AES-256). Access is gated by SSO/OAuth, scoped API tokens, and least-privilege RBAC. We log administrative actions and review access quarterly.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We use Vercel Analytics and HubSpot Analytics for product and marketing analytics, which may set cookies and similar technologies for usage measurement. We do not use advertising cookies or sell behavioral data.

9. Changes to this policy

We will provide advance notice — typically at least 30 days — before material changes take effect, by email or in-product notice.
