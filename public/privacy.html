1. What we collect

When you create an account: name, email, company, and (during checkout) billing details handled by Stripe — we do not store card numbers. When you connect data sources: CRM records, call recordings, and knowledge base content you authorize us to access. When you use the Service: usage logs, generated content, and product analytics.

2. How we use it

To provide the Service: read your data, generate enablement content, coach your reps, and surface pipeline insights. To bill you: process subscription payments via Stripe and Chargebee. To improve the Service: aggregate, anonymized usage analytics. We do not sell your data.

4. AI processing

Content you upload or connect is sent to Anthropic Claude (via Microsoft Azure AI Foundry) for inference. Per Anthropic's commercial terms via Azure, customer data is not used to train Anthropic models. Inference is performed in US Azure regions.

5. Data retention

Account data: retained while your subscription is active and for 90 days after cancellation, then deleted. Generated content: retained as long as your subscription is active; you can export at any time. Logs: 30 days. Backups: 35 days.

6. Your rights

You can request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal data by emailing dan@revplanner.io. We will respond within 30 days. Residents of California, the EU, and the UK have additional rights under CCPA, GDPR, and UK GDPR respectively.

7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (Azure Storage Service Encryption, AES-256). Access is gated by SSO/OAuth, scoped API tokens, and least-privilege RBAC. We log administrative actions and review access quarterly.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and analytics cookies (TBD: Plausible / PostHog) for product improvement. We do not use advertising cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

We will notify you by email at least 30 days before material changes take effect.
