Privacy Policy

Effective: July 20, 2026  ·  DLEA.IO LLC (operating as ProvenLoop)  ·  Terms of Service
Draft for publish — counsel review recommended. Not legal advice.

ProvenLoop is operated by DLEA.IO LLC, a Delaware limited liability company operating as ProvenLoop. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide: name, email, company, role.
  • Customer Data you connect or upload: CRM records, deal and pipeline data, contacts, and other business data from systems you authorize (e.g., HubSpot).
  • Usage information: how you interact with the Service, device and log data, and cookies (see §8).
  • Billing information: processed by our payment providers; we do not store full card numbers.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to: provide and operate the Service; analyze your connected data to produce insights and enablement content for you; personalize and improve the Service; communicate with you; process payments; maintain security; and comply with legal obligations.

4. AI Processing

Content you upload or connect to ProvenLoop is sent to our AI providers (currently Anthropic) for inference. Under our commercial agreements with these providers, your data is not used to train their models. Inference is performed in U.S. data centers. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. If you require regional data residency (e.g., EU-only processing), please contact us — this is a roadmap capability rather than a current commitment.

5. How We Share Information

We share information only: with subprocessors (§3) to operate the Service; to comply with law or valid legal process; to protect our rights, users, or the public; and in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice). We do not sell your personal information.

6. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal data by emailing privacy@provenloop.io. We respond within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30-45 days). Residents of jurisdictions with applicable privacy laws — including but not limited to California (CCPA/CPRA), Colorado (CPA), Virginia (VCDPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA), the EU and UK (GDPR/UK GDPR), and other states with comprehensive privacy legislation — have additional rights under those laws, including access, deletion, correction, opt-out of sale/sharing, and limitation of sensitive-data processing. To exercise these rights, email us and identify the jurisdiction whose law you are invoking.

7. Data Retention

We retain Customer Data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, and thereafter as required for legal, tax, or regulatory purposes or to resolve disputes. On termination, you may request export or deletion, subject to any legal-hold or retention requirements.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management (required for the app to work).

On this marketing site (provenloop.io), we use HubSpot Analytics (portal 246125112), which sets a cookie named hubspotutk. This cookie tracks your browsing session on the marketing site and is used to attribute form submissions (such as the beta application) to your session in HubSpot, so we can understand how visitors found us. The hubspotutk cookie persists for 13 months. HubSpot's privacy policy is available at legal.hubspot.com/privacy-policy.

We also use Vercel Web Analytics for aggregate site-traffic measurement. Vercel Web Analytics does not set persistent cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information.

We do not use advertising cookies. We do not sell behavioral data.

9. Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal data, including encryption of stored integration credentials and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.
